My Best Movies

Top 3 Series of all Time

Vikings

An intriguing story of a viking, Ragnar Lothbroke who had great ambition and vision far beyond what his viking people were used to. Ragnar rises from being an average villager to King of the East.

Peaky Blinders

A series about a London gang led by three brothers who in their quest for power, encounter difficulty and uprising but wittingly face them.

Money Heist

The intriguing story of a band of misfits brought together by a genius professor with the aim of hijacking Spain's money printing factory, printing money they can, and making away with it.
